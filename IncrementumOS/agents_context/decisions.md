## Investment philosophy

- Risk first, then returns. Capital preservation before everything.
- Druckenmiller-style: high-conviction concentration. Size bets when the edge is real.
- Portfolio rotates dynamically — agents read current positions from the Dashboard,
  not from memory or this file. When context about a decision is needed, ask.

## Thematic lens

The core question driving all investments:

> **Who are the winners in a changing geopolitical world and an emerging AI era?**

This applies across energy, memory, materials, defense, infrastructure — wherever the answer leads.
Themes are not fixed lists. They emerge from the macro and are validated bottom-up.

## Hedging

Dynamic and contextual. Eduardo hedges in different ways depending on the moment.
Agents do not assume a fixed hedge structure — ask when relevant.
