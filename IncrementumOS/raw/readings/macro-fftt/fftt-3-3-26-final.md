FFTT: So how are you positioning your own money for this, Mr. X, based on what you know? 

Mr. X: Western investor consensus appears to still think this will be over quickly, decisively in the US’ favor.  But I am 
hearing at the very least this is likely to take much longer than virtually anyone thinks right now, and it could be far 
messier than many think, with much more damage to energy infrastructure than most think.  As a result, I think oil goes

FFTT: What would be the first thing you would look to hear or see for a near-term bottom to stocks and risk
